LIVE at ~17:00Z — **4 hours before the scheduled 21:00Z kickoff** — which held
every surface at live-rate polling all afternoon and exhausted the 7,500/day
quota at 22:13Z, mid second half. Verified via prod endpoints: feed + DB frozen
on the last true snapshot; the live route's sim fallback stayed correctly
quarantined (nothing false displayed). Recovery was automatic at the 00:00Z
quota reset — self-heal wrote the real result (**FRA 4–6 ENG**, a 10-goal
epic) within minutes.

**Fix shipped (PR #172, gotcha #25): the kickoff-window quota guard.** Both
api-football entry points trust upstream LIVE only within
[kickoff−20m, kickoff+4.5h] of the fixture's OWN scheduled time; outside it,
statuses demote to NS and the live=all poll doesn't run at all. Worst-case
repeat of today = 20 minutes of early polling, not four hours. Final-day
budget: fresh 7,500, ~4.6k needed for a full-distance final.
