### PR #106 — knockout group-leak fix (from owner's POR–ESP screenshot)
Knockout matches inherit a residual `group` from the home team. That leaked three wrong
surfaces onto R16 pages: a 7-team "Group K standings" (knockout results POR 2-1 CRO and
COL 1-0 GHA were counted as group fixtures, dragging ESP/CRO/GHA in), a settled Group K
Prediction Markets panel, and knockout games in "Other Group K fixtures".
Fix: matches on/after `KNOCKOUT_START` never render group context; `buildGroupTable`
excludes knockout fixtures, so group tables are frozen at the group-stage final state.
